  • Sofia the First

    Franchise

    Sofia the First (2013-2018)

    Associated Color

    Lavender

    Profile

    Princess of Enchancia, daughter of Birk Balthazar and Queen Miranda, stepsister of Princess Amber and Prince James. She lived as a peasant with her mother, Miranda, before she married into the Royal Family and became a Princess. She possesses the Amulet of Avalor, which allows her to summon Disney Princesses to help her.

  • Loid Forger

    Profile

  • Steve Rogers

    Franchise

    Marvel Cinematic Universe (2011-)

    Associated Color

    Blue

    Profile

    Popularly known as "Captain America", World War II veteran, recepient of the Super Soldier Serum, member of the Avengers. Former member of SHIELD and major participant at the Battle of Sokovia, the conflict against Thanos, and the Battle of Earth. He surrendered his shield to Sam Wilson at the end of the Avengers phase of the MCU and retired.



  • Cassian Andor

    Franchise

    Star Wars Universe (1977-)

    Associated Color

    Brown

    Profile

    A male operative and member of the Alliance to Restore the Republic, he was born on the Free Trade sector world of Ferrix and lived their with his family, eventually becoming a spy, assassin, soldier and pilot. He participated in the effort to secure Imperial plans for the Death Star from Galen Erso and the subsequent Battle of Scarif, where he also passed away. His sacrifice allowed the Alliance to successfully destroy the Death Star, allowing for the rise of the democratic New Republic.
